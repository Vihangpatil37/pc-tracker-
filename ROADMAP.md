# FocusOS Roadmap

FocusOS is designed to be the premier open-source digital wellbeing application for Windows. Our primary goal for v1.0 was to build a rock-solid, 100% local activity tracking foundation.

Looking ahead, we are excited to expand the feature set without compromising our privacy-first principles.

## Next Up (v1.1 - v1.2)

- **Data Export**: Allow users to export their activity data easily.
  - Export to `.csv` for spreadsheet analysis.
  - Export to `.json` for programmatic access.
  - 1-click SQLite database backup.
- **Categorization Rules**: Automatically tag applications.
  - Define rules to classify apps into categories like *Development*, *Gaming*, *Learning*, *Entertainment*, and *Work*.
  - View dashboard statistics grouped by category.
- **Privacy Mode**: A quick toggle to hide sensitive window titles from the dashboard and logs when sharing screenshots or screens.

## Future Exploration (v2.0+)

- **Weekly Automated Reports**: Generate beautiful HTML/PDF weekly summaries of your screen time directly on your machine.
- **Advanced Search**: Search through thousands of historical sessions by application name or exact window title.
- **Session Replay Timeline**: A highly interactive, zoomable chart displaying your exact activity throughout the day.
- **Idle Timeout Customization**: Allow users to configure exactly how many seconds of zero mouse/keyboard input triggers an "idle" state.
